# Explanation Definition
This quadrant goes by a variety of other names, such as ‘Context’ or ’Discussion’.

## Notes
* Should be for people new to meshtastic trying to get up to speed.
* Should teach new information but not say what to do with the information 
* Should generally be for answering a "why do we do ____?"

## Examples
* A page that explains why we don't buy antennas from Amazon
* A page that explains why height matters so much for antennas
